# Crop Yield Data Description:
- Seven prediction tasks: `['Barley', 'Maize', 'Millet', 'Rice, paddy', 'Sorghum', 'Wheat']`
- For each prediction task:
    - Four features: `[temp, wind, prcp, rain]`
    - One label: `crop_yield`
